Toolbar

Acts as a navigation bar.

The next element needs to be the container for the main data as the toolbar automatically adds a margin-top to its next element.

USE

toolbar|[seamed|scroll]

+ left

+ right

+ title

seamed — Removes the toolbar shadow.

scroll — Makes the toolbar scroll with the content

left — An icon at the left side of the toolbar.

right — An icon at the right side of the toolbar.

title — The title of the current page. This will change automatically when changing pages(see pages).
